## Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS)

### Small Tactical Drones
**Real-world:** Switchblade, Hero-series, Lancet
**NEXUS equivalent:** HORNET (loitering munition)

| Spec | Real-World | Game Value |
|------|------------|------------|
| Endurance | 15-40 min | ~30 min |
| Range | 10-40 km | 25 km |
| Payload | 1-3 kg warhead | One-shot kill on soft targets |
| Sensors | EO/IR | EO/IR (targeting) |

## Design Principle

NEXUS platforms are **abstractions** that capture the essential gameplay characteristics of real systems without simulating specific real-world platforms. This allows:
- Fictional setting freedom
- Balance without realism debates
- Near-future speculation
- Avoiding political sensitivity

The goal: **feel real** without **being real**.
